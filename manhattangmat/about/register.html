What is a Computer Adaptive Test (CAT)?

GMAT registration varies depending on the country in which you live. In the United States, you can register to take the GMAT by phone or mail. The GMAT registration fee in the United States is currently $165. You may schedule your test just a few days before taking it, but keep in mind that certain dates and times are filled quickly so the earlier you register, the better chance you have of securing the date of your choice.

To register by phone call 1-800-GMAT-NOW (1-800-462-8669).

For complete registration information visit the Official GMAT Website.

You may also write to the Educational Testing Service, the organization that administers the GMAT.
