Manhattan GMAT is committed to meeting the
specific needs of your company.
*Meeting times are scheduled for the convenience of your employees. Typically, classes are held once a week on any Sunday through Thursday evening.

*Class size varies depending on the needs of your company. A minimum of 8 students is required to sponsor a Corporate Class.

*Curricular emphasis is determined by the needs of your employees. For example, if a highly math-intensive section is desired, we will provide it.
*All classes are videotaped so students can make up any class they may have missed!
