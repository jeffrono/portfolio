One amazing statistic attesting to this fact is that the average student accepted by a top 15 business school today has scored in the 90th percentile or better on the GMAT.

Our mission at Manhattan GMAT is to provide you with the skills and strategies essential for success, given today's higher standards for what defines a competitive GMAT score.
